About Irish History Tales

Irish History Tales is a long-form journey through Ireland’s past, told in short videos and historical fiction.

The Channel

On TikTok and YouTube, Irish History Tales breaks down complex events into clear, chronological episodes – perfect for students, teachers, and anyone curious about Irish history.

  • Series on rebellions, revolutions & key figures
  • Short explainers ideal for classroom use
  • Content aligned with exam topics where possible

Darren Beaming

Darren Beaming writes historical fiction that intertwines real events with character-driven stories. His work on the United Irishmen era inspired Irish History Tales and vice versa.

  • Author of Verses in the Sand
  • Focus on late 18th-century & Napoleonic periods
  • Blending accurate research with accessible storytelling
