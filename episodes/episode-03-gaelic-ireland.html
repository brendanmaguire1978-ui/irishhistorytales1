Episode 3

Gaelic Ireland: Kingdoms, Tanistry, Rivalries & High Kings

Published 14 April 2026

Episode overview

An exploration of how power operated in twelfth-century Gaelic Ireland, including its kingdoms, dynasties, social structure, tanistry and the contested authority of the High Kingship.

This episode provides the political framework needed for the invasion story by explaining how Gaelic kingdoms, dynasties and succession operated before the Normans arrived.

Topics covered

  • The kingdoms and dynasties of Gaelic Ireland
  • Tanistry and succession within ruling families
  • Social and political structures in the twelfth century
  • The limited and contested authority of the High Kingship
