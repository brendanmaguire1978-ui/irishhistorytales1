Episode 7

The Fight for Dublin, 1171: The Norse-Gaelic Attack on Dublin

Published 6 May 2026

Episode overview

After Dermot MacMurrough’s death, Strongbow claims Leinster through Aoife. Ascall Mac Torcaill returns by sea to reclaim Dublin, leading to a final struggle for the old Norse-Gaelic kingdom.

This episode returns to Dublin after Dermot’s death and follows the Norse-Gaelic effort to recover the city from Strongbow and his supporters.

Topics covered

  • Strongbow’s claim to Leinster through Aoife
  • Ascall Mac Torcaill’s return by sea
  • The Norse-Gaelic attempt to recover Dublin
  • The final struggle for the old Hiberno-Norse kingdom
