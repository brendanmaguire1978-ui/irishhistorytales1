Introduction to Irish History: The Gaelic Era & Primary Sources

Published 4 April 2026

Episode overview

The opening episode introduces the chronological series, the Gaelic world, early Christianity and the principal sources used to reconstruct medieval Irish history.

The opening episode establishes the chronological method of the series and introduces the sources, institutions and cultural setting needed to understand the story that follows.

Topics covered

  • The purpose and chronological structure of the series
  • The Gaelic world before the Norman invasion
  • Early Christianity in Ireland
  • The primary sources used to reconstruct medieval history
