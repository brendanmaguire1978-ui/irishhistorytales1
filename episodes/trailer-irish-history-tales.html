Episode overview

An introduction to the chronological podcast journey through Irish history, from the Gaelic world and Viking Ireland to the Norman invasion and the political struggles that followed.

The trailer introduces the scope, style and chronological method of Irish History Tales, preparing listeners for a long-form journey through Ireland’s political and social history.

Topics covered

  • The chronological approach of Irish History Tales
  • Gaelic Ireland and the medieval world
  • Viking settlement and Hiberno-Norse Ireland
  • The Norman invasion and the political struggles that followed
