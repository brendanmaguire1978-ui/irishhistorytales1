Episode 8

The Siege of Dublin, 1171: The High King of Ireland’s Great Gaelic Siege of Norman Dublin

Published 13 May 2026

Episode overview

Rory O’Connor surrounds Dublin with a great Gaelic coalition while the Norman garrison runs short of food. With negotiations failing, Strongbow, Raymond le Gros and Milo de Cogan launch a desperate sortie.

This companion page explores the great siege of 1171, when Rory O’Connor attempted to contain the Normans in Dublin and force a settlement before a sudden breakout changed the campaign.

Topics covered

  • Rory O’Connor’s coalition around Dublin
  • The food crisis inside the Norman-held city
  • Failed negotiations between the opposing forces
  • The desperate sortie led by the Norman commanders
