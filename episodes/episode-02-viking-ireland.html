Episode 2

Viking Ireland: Dublin’s “Millennium”, the Battle of Clontarf & the Hiberno-Norse

Published 10 April 2026

Episode overview

The Vikings evolve from raiders into settlers, traders and city builders. The episode examines the Battle of Clontarf, Dublin’s disputed millennium and the emergence of Hiberno-Norse Ireland.

This page follows the long Viking presence in Ireland and explains how Scandinavian communities became part of a distinctive Hiberno-Norse urban and political world.

Topics covered

  • The transformation of Vikings from raiders to settlers
  • Dublin as a trading and urban centre
  • The Battle of Clontarf and its later reputation
  • The emergence of Hiberno-Norse Ireland
