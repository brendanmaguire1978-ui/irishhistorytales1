Strongbow Before Ireland: The Early Life of Richard de Clare

Published 19 June 2026

Episode overview

A biography of Richard de Clare before the invasion of Ireland, exploring his family, upbringing during the Anarchy, connections to the Welsh Marches and difficult relationship with Henry II.

This biographical bonus episode steps back from the invasion narrative to examine the experiences, family connections and political difficulties that shaped Richard de Clare before Ireland.

Topics covered

  • Richard de Clare’s family and early life
  • Strongbow’s background during the Anarchy
  • Connections with the Welsh Marches
  • His difficult relationship with Henry II
