Episode 5

The Norman Conquest of Leinster: The Battle of Baginbun, Sieges of Waterford & Dublin

Published 22 April 2026

Episode overview

The first Norman forces gain a foothold in Ireland. Raymond le Gros survives the Battle of Baginbun before Strongbow arrives to take Waterford and Dublin, transforming a mercenary expedition into a conquest.

This episode covers the decisive phase in which small Norman expeditionary forces captured major centres and turned an invitation for military assistance into a territorial conquest.

Topics covered

  • The first Norman foothold in Ireland
  • Raymond le Gros and the Battle of Baginbun
  • The capture of Waterford
  • The seizure of Dublin and transformation of the invasion
